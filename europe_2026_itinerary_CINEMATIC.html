✓ Quick safe / ✗ avoid guide
Safe Grilled meat & fish, risotto (ask — no flour), polenta, salads, cheese, cured meats (check), eggs, fruit, gelato (fruit sorbets + most cream; skip cone & biscuit flavours), socca (Nice), wine, coffee.
Ask Sauces & gravies (flour-thickened?), fried items (shared oil), soups, processed meats, "GF pasta" (confirm separate water).
Avoid Bread, regular pasta, pizza, gnocchi, breaded/crumbed dishes, beer, pastries, pissaladière, anything battered.
